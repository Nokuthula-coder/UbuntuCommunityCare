Welcome to Ubuntu Community Care

Founded in 2017, Ubuntu Community Care has been committed to uplifting vulnerable communities by providing hope, support, and opportunities for a better future. Our mission is to build a self-sustaining community where every individual has access to basic human needs, education, and empowerment opportunities. Through partnerships, donations, and volunteer work, we continue to make a positive impact in the lives of children, families, and elderly community members.

Our Programs

We are committed to making a meaningful difference through three core pillars:

Food Parcel Distribution

We provide weekly and emergency food parcels, nutrition education, and holiday food drives to vulnerable families in our community.

Educational Support

Supporting children's education through after-school programs, tutoring, school supply drives, and bursary assistance.

Community Development & Skills Training

Empowering communities through skills training, small-community infrastructure projects, and family empowerment workshops.
