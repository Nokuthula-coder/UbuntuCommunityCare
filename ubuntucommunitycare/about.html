About Ubuntu Community Care

 UbuntuCommunity Care is a non-profit organization established in 2017in Siyabuswa. Our foundation is rooted in the African philosophy of Ubuntu- “I am because we are”- the belief that humanity is built through connection,compassion and shared responsibility. We exist to support underprivileged families through emergency food relief, education support, and create opportunities that restore dignity and hope.

Our mission, vision, values

 Mission: To improve the quality of life for underprivileged families in Siyabuswa by providing food security, educational opportunities, and community development resources.

 Vision:To build a self-reliant, empowered community where every individual has access to basic needs,children can pursue education, and communities thrive through shared resources and skills.

 Corevalues: Ubuntu&Solidarity — we work together with dignity and respect. Transparency — we manage resources responsibly and share our results. Empowerment—weequip people with skills for self-sufficiency. Inclusivity — we support all community members without discrimination
