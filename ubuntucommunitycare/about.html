Frequently Asked Questions

Ubuntu Community Care is a non-profit organization based in Siyabuswa, Our foundation is rooted in the African philosophy of Ubuntu- “I am because we are”- the belief that humanity is built through connection,compassion and shared responsibility. We exist to support underprivileged families through food assistance, education programs, volunteer opportunities, and community development resources.

Complete the enquiry form and our team will contact you.

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for underprivileged families in Siyabuswa by providing food security, educational opportunities, and community development resources.

Our Vision

To build a self-reliant, empowered community where every individual has access to basic needs, children can pursue education, and communities thrive through shared resources and skills.

Our Core Values

Ubuntu&Solidarity — we work together with dignity and respect.
Transparency — we manage resources responsibly and share our results.
Empowerment — we equip people with skills for self-sufficiency.
Inclusivity — we support all community members without discrimination

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff are the heart of Ubuntu Community Care. We are passionate about making a difference and work tirelessly to support our community.

From organizing food distributions to tutoring students, our team is committed to creating positive change in Siyabuswa.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have provided over 10,000 food parcels to families in need, supported 200+ children with educational resources, and facilitated vocational training for 50+ community members.

We are proud of the positive impact we have made and are committed to expanding our reach and deepening our impact in the years to come.

Why Our Work Matters

In a community where many struggle to meet basic needs, our work provides critical support that can change lives. Food security is essential for health and well-being, and education is a key pathway out of poverty. Together with our supporters, we continue building hope for a brighter future.
