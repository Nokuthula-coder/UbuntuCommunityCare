Community Programs

Ubuntu Community Care provides various community support programs aimed at improving the lives of disadvantaged individuals and families.

Food parcels & nutrition

Weekly distributions, emergency response, nutrition education sessions, holiday drives.

Education & youth support

After-school clubs, homework help, school supplies and uniforms,bursary referrals, life-skills workshops.

Community development & skills

Vocational training (e.g., sewing, carpentry), micro-enterprise mentoring, small community infrastructure (community gardens, safe play spaces)
